Saffron Impala

Features

The Saffron Impala was a scientist, who got exposed to some chemicals while conducting his research. The chemicals had a great impact on him. The notable features of Saffron Impala are listed below.

  1. When he gets angry, he turns into Saffron color.
  2. When he is Saffron, his bones and muscles grow in size, tearing the clothes he had put on.
  3. The Saffron Impala can jump very high.
  4. The Saffron Impala is very strong.
  5. The Saffron Impala is immortal.
